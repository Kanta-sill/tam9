Hunting wild animals issues

Hunting is the seeking, pursuance and capture or destruction of game and wild animals for subsistence, profit or sport. Most governments establish "open" and "closed" hunting seasons and require hunters to be licensed. However, in some parts of the world the number of individuals wishing to hunt as a sport and who take advantage of the open season is increasing to such an extent that problems are being created.
